Tesseract

Parth Bapat


Tesseract is an open-source optical character recognition (OCR) engine developed by Google. It is written in C++ and can be used to recognize text in images and convert it into machine-readable text. Tesseract is considered one of the most accurate OCR engines currently available, and it supports a wide range of languages and scripts.
