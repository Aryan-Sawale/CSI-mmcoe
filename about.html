About CSI MMCOE

We help students to become a better version of themselves in all aspects.
Our mission is to make students research oriented, and to enhance their knowledge about various new domains and help them shape their careers. CSI provides a platform to nurture their skills.
It broadcasts/showcases the benefits of IT advancements to all sections of Indian Society.
The objective of CSI Students' Chapter is to be a professional society where students can meet, exchange views/information and learn to share ideas.
